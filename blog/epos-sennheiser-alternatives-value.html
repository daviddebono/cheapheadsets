EPOS and Sennheiser Alternatives: Value Options

EPOS and Sennheiser are known for premium audio. Here we look at value alternatives that can suit business use.

Summary

EPOS (formerly Sennheiser Communications) offers premium sound and technologies like BrainAdapt™. Legacy Sennheiser enterprise headsets are still in use. Budget alternatives from Mairdi and others can provide good sound quality for typical calls and collaboration at lower cost.

About EPOS and Sennheiser

EPOS draws on Sennheiser’s audiophile heritage and is known for superior sound quality and technologies that reduce cognitive load during long calls. They have strong positioning in call centres (IMPACT series) and hybrid work (ADAPT series). Sennheiser’s legacy enterprise headsets are still widely used; the 2020 split left EPOS as the main enterprise audio brand. Both command premium prices.

Frequently asked questions

Are EPOS and Sennheiser the same?

EPOS was formed from Sennheiser’s former communications division. Sennheiser enterprise headsets are now largely represented through EPOS.

Do budget headsets match EPOS sound quality?

Premium brands typically offer refined audio. Many budget headsets provide acceptable clarity for voice and collaboration; compare specs and reviews for your use case.
