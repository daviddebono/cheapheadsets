Plantronics and Poly Alternatives on a Budget

Plantronics (now Poly by HP) is a long-standing leader in professional headsets. Here we look at budget alternatives that can meet similar needs.

Summary

Poly headsets (Savi, Voyager and others) are known for comfort, durability and noise cancellation. Value alternatives from Mairdi and similar brands can provide reliable performance for offices and call centres at a lower price. Bulk orders and discounts are available.

About Plantronics and Poly

Plantronics has been a pioneer in professional headsets since 1961. Now under the Poly by HP brand, they offer wireless and wired models including the Savi and Voyager series. They are recognised for comfort, durability and advanced noise cancellation and have deep enterprise presence in call centres and offices. If you want similar functionality without the premium price, budget alternatives are worth considering.

What to look for in an alternative

Match your use case: wireless vs wired, over-ear vs on-ear, and compatibility with your phone system or computer. Check microphone quality, battery life (for wireless), and comfort for long wear. Frequently asked questions

Are Poly and Plantronics the same?

Poly is the current brand (Poly by HP); Plantronics was the former company name. Many people still refer to Plantronics when discussing Poly headsets.
