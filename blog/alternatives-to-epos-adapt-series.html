The EPOS ADAPT series focuses on hybrid work with long battery life and multi-device connectivity. Here are value alternatives.

EPOS delivers premium audio and technologies that reduce cognitive load during long calls. The ADAPT series is aimed at hybrid workers with strong battery life and multi-device support. Frequently asked questions

What is multipoint?

Multipoint lets one headset stay connected to two devices (e.g. laptop and phone) and switch between them for calls and audio.
