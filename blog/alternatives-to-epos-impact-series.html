Alternatives to the EPOS IMPACT Series Call Centre Headsets

The EPOS IMPACT series is designed for call centres with ANC and all-day comfort. Here are value alternatives.

Summary

EPOS (formerly Sennheiser Communications) offers audiophile-grade engineering and technologies like BrainAdapt™. The IMPACT series targets call centres with active noise cancellation and comfort for long shifts. Budget USB and wireless alternatives can deliver reliable performance for contact centres at lower cost. We offer bulk orders and discounts across Australian capitals.

Why consider an alternative?

EPOS IMPACT headsets are premium-priced. If you need clear voice, noise reduction and comfortable wear for high-volume use without the IMPACT price, value options from Mairdi and similar brands are worth comparing. See our USB headsets and wireless headphones for product details and links to view and purchase.

Bulk and other hardware

We offer bulk orders and bulk discounts. For other hardware—switches, routers, phone systems or handsets—our partner Telco Broker can assist; contact us and mention your needs.

Frequently asked questions

Do budget headsets have active noise cancellation?

Many budget models offer passive isolation and microphone noise reduction. Full ANC is more common on higher-priced headsets.
