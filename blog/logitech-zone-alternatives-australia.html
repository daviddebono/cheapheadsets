Logitech Zone headsets are popular for video calls. Here are value alternatives for Australian buyers.

Summary

Logitech Zone models (e.g. Zone 950) offer comfortable, reliable business headsets with good microphone noise cancellation and strong integration with video tools. Budget alternatives can deliver similar usability for Teams, Zoom and softphones at lower cost.

About Logitech Zone

Logitech is known for comfortable, reliable business headsets with excellent microphone noise cancellation. The Zone 950 and related models are widely used for video collaboration.
