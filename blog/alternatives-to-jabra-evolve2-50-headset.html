Alternatives to the Jabra Evolve2 50 Headset

The Jabra Evolve2 50 is a leading wired office headset. Here are value-focused alternatives for Australian businesses.

Summary

Jabra is known for advanced microphone noise-reduction and strong wireless connectivity, with a dominant position in enterprise audio. The Evolve2 50 is a top wired pick for offices. Budget alternatives from Mairdi and similar brands can deliver solid USB performance at lower cost. We offer bulk orders and bulk discounts across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart.

Why consider an alternative?

Jabra headsets command a premium. If you need clear voice, comfort and reliable USB connectivity without the Evolve2 50 price tag, value options are worth comparing. Many offices and call centres in Australia standardise on one or two models and order in bulk—we can provide quotes for USB headsets that suit desk-based and hybrid work.

What to look for

Look for plug-and-play USB, noise-reducing microphone, comfortable fit for long wear, and compatibility with Teams, Zoom and your softphone. For other hardware such as switches, routers or phone systems, our partner Telco Broker can assist—mention your needs on our contact page.
