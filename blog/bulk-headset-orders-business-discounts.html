Bulk Headset Orders and Business Discounts

How businesses can save when buying headsets in volume.

Summary

We offer bulk orders and bulk discounts on Bluetooth, wireless, USB and telephone headsets. Standardising on one or two models simplifies procurement and support. Contact us for a quote—we service all major Australian cities and suburbs.

Why order in bulk?

Ordering headsets in bulk reduces per-unit cost, simplifies procurement and makes it easier to standardise across the team. One or two SKUs mean simpler training, spare stock and replacement. We offer bulk discounts for businesses in Sydney (including Parramatta, North Sydney, Chatswood), Melbourne (Richmond, South Yarra, Docklands), Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra and Hobart and their main suburbs.

What we offer

We provide information and purchase links for Bluetooth headsets, wireless headphones, USB headsets and telephone headsets. For bulk orders we can provide tailored quotes and, where relevant, connect you with our partner. Contact us with your headcount and requirements. If you need other hardware—switches, routers, phone systems or handsets—our partner Telco Broker can assist.
