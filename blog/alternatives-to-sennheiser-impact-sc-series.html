Alternatives to Sennheiser Impact SDW and SC Series Business Headsets

Sennheiser Impact SDW and SC series are legacy enterprise headsets still used in comparisons. Here are value alternatives.

Summary

Sennheiser is historically recognised for high-quality wired and wireless business headsets. The Impact SDW (5036, 5066) and SC series (SC45, SC230, SC632) still appear in enterprise comparisons. Enterprise business is now largely represented through EPOS. Budget alternatives from Mairdi and others can deliver solid performance at lower cost. Sennheiser legacy enterprise models command premium prices. If you need reliable wired or wireless business audio without the Sennheiser price tag, value options are available. See our USB headsets, telephone headsets and Bluetooth headsets for product details and links to view and purchase.
