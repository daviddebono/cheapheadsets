Privacy Policy

This policy describes how we collect and use information when you use our website cheapheadsets.com.au.

Information we collect

When you submit the contact or enquiry form, we collect the information you provide: name, email, phone and message. When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. We may also receive technical data such as IP address and browser type from our hosting and form provider.

How we use it

We use enquiry information to respond to your request and to follow up on bulk orders or product questions. We use newsletter email addresses to send updates about products and offers. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Form submissions

Form submissions are processed by a third-party form provider (e.g. Formspree). Their privacy policy applies to data transmitted through their service. Submissions are forwarded to our team so we can respond.

Cookies

Our site may use minimal cookies or similar technology for essential operation (e.g. session). We do not use advertising cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. If you are in Australia, we comply with applicable Australian privacy law. If you are in the European Economic Area, you may have additional rights under GDPR.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always on this page. Last updated: February 2025.
